In 69% of baladiyas, IDPs were reported to purchase food from the market as their main source of food, representing no changes in percentage from the previous round. The proportion of IDPs obtaining food on credit was reported to be 17% representing no changes from the previous round. In 13% of baladiyas the main source of food was reported to be from charity or donations and in the remaining 1% of baladiyas the main source of food was from family or friends. The main problem associated with access to food was that it was too expensive as reported in 96 assessed baladiyas. Cases of malnutrition were reported in 17% of baladiyas and were reported to be present in the South and West of the country. It was also reported in one baladiya in the East, Benghazi.
