[5-13th April 2021, NES] In April, 87% of vendors reported facing supply challenges. The primary supply challenge cited remained price inflation, reported by 75% of all surveyed vendors in Northeast Syria (NES). As a seemingly widespread supply challenge, price inflation issues may also impact vendors’ ability to keep markets stocked with essential items. The second cited issue reported was that supply was unable to keep up with consumer demand, reported by 24% of all surveyed vendors. Furthermore, 15% of surveyed vendors reported that their suppliers had limited supply, and 9% reported that their supply had been affected by border crossing closures.
