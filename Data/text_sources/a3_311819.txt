Overall, 7.2 million people were estimated to be in need of urgent humanitarian assistance. This represents a 65 per cent increase compared to 2019. Major concern is the 10 out of the 18 states of Sudan, with acute food insecurity are South Kordofan (38 per cent of the state population are food insecure), Blue Nile (34 per cent food insecure), North Darfur (32 per cent food insecure), Central Darfur (30 per cent food insecure), West Darfur (30 per cent food insecure), Red Sea (28 per cent food insecure), Kassala (25 per cent food insecure), South Darfur (21 per cent food insecure), North Kordofan (21 per cent food insecure) and East Darfur (20 per cent food insecure).
