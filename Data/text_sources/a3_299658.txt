[March 28, As-Sweida] The head of the School Health Department in Swaida, Dr. Khair Abu Fakhr, also indicated that the total number of confirmed cases of Coronavirus that is confirmed by laboratory analysis in schools has increased to 11 cases since the start of the second semester after recording two new infections last week. He explained that the 9 infections were among the educational and administrative staff, and two injuries among the students, indicating that three injuries recovered, while the remaining eight injuries are under home quarantine, in addition to the presence of 15 suspected cases that were recently recorded.
