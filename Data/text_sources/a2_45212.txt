In Damboa, nearly three in four households included an adult with no identity documentation (70%), the highest across the state. One-fifth of households also reported missing a family member (19%), the third highest across the state, and 44% of households reported experiencing a security incident the two months prior to data collection, also the highest across the state. In Mafa, one in five households included adults without identity documentation (21%), and one in four experienced movement restrictions (28%). Magumeri included one of the one of the highest rates of wards where key informants had reported previous explosive incidents (86%).
