[28th December 2020, Bangladesh] 1,772 Rohingyas are expected to travel to their new home in Bhashan Char on Tuesday, joining 1,645 already living there Buoyed by the success of the first relocation over three weeks ago, the government is all set to transfer more than 1,000 Rohingyas from the camps in Cox's Bazar to Bhashan Char in Noakhali on Tuesday. These persecuted people will join with 1,645 of their fellow citizens, who were relocated to the island in the Bay of Bengal on December 4.Actually, 1,642 Rohingyas arrived in Bhashan Char on that day. Since then, three babies were born to take the number to 1,645.
