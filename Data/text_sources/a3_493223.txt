[July 2021, Overall Syria] The price of flour recorded a slight 1 percent m-o-m decrease and the average selling price reached 1 726 SYP per kg. The prices in Rural Damascus, Dará and Deir-ez-Zor remained constant in July 2021, with Aleppo governorate recording a slight decrease (3 percent m-o-m), with the selling price reaching 1 750 SYP per kg. Hama, Homs, Tartous and Hasakeh governorates recorded a slight increase (9, 8, 7 and 2 percent m-o-m) with the selling prices reaching 1 800, 1 780, 1 800 and 1 325 SYP per kg respectively
