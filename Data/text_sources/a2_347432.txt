[1st January 2021, North-East, Nigeria]From the results, more than half of the respondents (55.38%) said they stayed in the reception centre before they (were) relocated to camps, while 44.62 per cent said they have never stayed in the reception centre. 91.09 per cent of respondents who once stayed in the reception centre confirmed that they lived in the reception centre for more than three days after arrival, 5.30 per cent said they stayed in the reception centre between one to two days, while 156 (3.61%) of the respondents said they stayed in the reception centre for about 72 hours before they were relocated to camps.
