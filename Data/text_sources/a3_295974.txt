With screening staff operating at the Herat and Nimroz border crossing points and case workers in 11 provinces of return, the Protection team caseload this week included case management assessments for 60 new PSN households in Nimroz, Ghor, Balkh, Kunduz, Herat, Badakhshan, Takhar, Badakhshan and Sar-e-Pol. These cases included 8 children at risk, 5 people with serious medical conditions, 4 women at risk, 3 persons with disabilities, and 3 older persons at risk. Across all provinces, 2,062 cases are ongoing. 130 PSN individuals (and their households) were identified at the Milak and Islam Qala border points by Protection screeners in coordination with DoRR.
