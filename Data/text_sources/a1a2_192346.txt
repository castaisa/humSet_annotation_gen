[July-August 2020, Bangladesh] Host Community: Compared to the findings of the 2019 J-MSNA26, the proportion of households reporting receiving remittances decreased by more than half from 9% to 4%. At the same time, the proportion of households indicating other forms of assistance from friends/relatives as a source of income increased from 7% to 12%, while the proportion of households reporting savings as a source of income increased from 2% to 10%. While the proportion of households with income-earners as well as the proportion of individuals earning an income remained comparable to 2019, 93% of households did report diminished income or loss of income sources as an impact of the COVID-19 outbreak
