[27th Oct 2020,North East Nigeria]In Nigeria, women’s access to mobile phones and the internet is high. 84% of women in the country own mobile phones and there is only a 5% gender gap between men and women’s access. However, only 35% of women have access to internet as compared to 50% of men10. This gap, coupled with family disapproval of women having access to a phone and internet, widens the gap in Borno. Because of the pandemic, women have begun accessing mobile phones and the internet which overall, they report to be happy with this change. However, a majority of male respondents reported this to be a negative change as they fear that it will distract women from their domestic work.
