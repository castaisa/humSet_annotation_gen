In line with commitments made in the 2020 HRP to extend activities that support people to recover and build their resilience, Food Security and Agriculture Cluster (FSAC) partners were able to mobilise communal asset creation assistance to 122,269 people at an estimated cost of $4.1 million. This included building of small dams to retain rain water for subsequent irrigation of cash and staple crops. It also included building of irrigation canals that allow communities to retain and manage water flows, mitigate soil erosion, reduce waste of water, and increase the access to water for communities living away from water sources.
