[28th December 2020, Bangladesh] The government will arrange for quarantine facilities at Ashkona Hajj Camp and Uttara Diabari, but the passenger will have to bear all the expenses. Also, the Civil Aviation Authority Bangladesh (CAAB) might arrange good hotels for quarantine if demanded by the passengers, he added. Responding to a question, the cabinet secretary said institutional quarantine will be mandatory even if a passenger brings negative report collected a day ago. A notification will be issued for the enforcement of the quarantine rule after a logical time of two-three days, said Secretary Khandker Anwarul. Later in the day, a virtual meeting chaired by the cabinet secretary, decided that this move will be implemented from January 1, 2021.
