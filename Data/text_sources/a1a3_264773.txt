[1st Oct 2020,North east Nigeria]COVID-related restrictions as well as wider economic impact negatively affected 63.5 percent of all households. This aggregated figure for negative economic impact is further broken down as follows: 17.6 percent all households mentioning complete loss of income source, 30 percent deterioration in income, 15.9 percent a disproportionate increase in the cost of living. The remaining 35 percent of all households witnessed no difference or a change in income source, while a minority 1.8 percent mentioned that it has improved income.
