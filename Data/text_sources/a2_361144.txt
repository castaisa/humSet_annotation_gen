The data also showed that 10.4% of men have more than one job, while this percentage among women does not exceed 2%. This is despite the decline in living standards in Syria, and the high percentage of the population living below the poverty line. The data collected also shows that 45.3% of female respondents stated that their reason for being unemployed was full-time housework, while none of the male respondents chose this option.
