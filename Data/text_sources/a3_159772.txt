Furthermore, UNICEF supported water treatment through utilization monitoring of 450 drums of (45 kg) chlorine (HTH) donated to Adamawa State Government and 873 drums of (45 kg) chlorine (HTH) donated to Borno State Governmentbetween January and April. In June, another five drums were released to for COVID-19/cholera response in Monguno LGA, while 10 drums and sprayers were provided for use in 10 health/CMAM centres in Askira Uba, Biu, Chibok, Hawul, Kwaya Kusar, Gwoza and Shani LGAs of Borno State. UNICEF also provided two drums of Calcium Hypochlorite (HTH) and soaps and installed 20 handwashing facilities in Maiduguri International Airport to mitigate the transmission of COVID-19 in Borno, benefitting up to 2,000 persons
