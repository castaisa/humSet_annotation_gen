During the past week a total of 17,772 patients were served by IOM’s 7 mobile health teams, inclusive of 3,142 outpatient consultations, health education sessions for 14,630 persons and psychosocial counselling sessions for 607 patients. Under the Global Fund multi-country TB grant in partnership with UNDP, a total of 10,954 undocumented Afghans were screened for tuberculosis at the Zero Point Reception Centres of Herat, Nimroz, Kandahar and Nangarhar. Of the total screened, 235 presumptive TB cases were identified. Samples from the presumptive cases were lab tested through GeneXpert and normal microscopy in coordination with the National TB Control Programme, and 1 person was confirmed positive for TB.
