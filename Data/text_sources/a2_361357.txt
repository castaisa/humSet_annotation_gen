[21 June 2021, Cox's Bazar] Social context: Half of the children died whilst with one or more friends of a similar age, whilst in nine (45%) cases of fatal drowning, the child was alone, and in an individual case of fatal drowning, the child was with an adult who was a relative. The social context in which the fatalities occurred varied between age groups. Amongst children under 5, the dominant theme was that the child was playing inside or around their family shelter. During this time, the victim’s parents were undertaking essential household tasks such as preparing food, work, and prayer, which meant that the child was unsupervised and fell into nearby water bodies, or water sources inside their shelter.
