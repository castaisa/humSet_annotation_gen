[November 2, Idleb] The director of Al-Mafraq camp in northern Idlib, Abu Nawaf, explains that the amount of water available per person ranges between 30 or 35 liters per day.
For bathing days, the available quantities of water become greater, and the water allocated to one family is barely enough, which causes a crisis that leads families to rationing the use of water, according to Mustafa Ali Al-Muhammad, who is displaced from the countryside of Maarat al-Numan.
The director of the "Syria Response Coordinators" team, Muhammad Hallaj, told the "Syria TV" website that the lack of water in the camps is one of the main reasons for the spread of Corona virus among the displaced.
