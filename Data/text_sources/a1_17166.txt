Resettlement processing in Libya (Group 1) Since 1 September 2017, 1,064 individuals have been submitted for resettlement to 7 States (Canada, France, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Sweden, Switzerland). A total of 318 individuals have departed on resettlement from Libya directly or through the ETC in Romania to Canada, France, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway and Sweden. A total of 130 individuals have been accepted for resettlement and are pending departure from Libya directly or through the ETC Romania to Canada (38), France (4), the Netherlands (1), Norway (81) and Sweden (6). A total of 638 individuals have been submitted for resettlement and are pending interview or decision by a resettlement State in Libya.
