[Following conflict in West Darfur on 3-4 April 2021] A multifunctional humanitarian team made up of the National Commission for the Reception and Reintegration of Refugees and Returnees (CNARR), UNHCR, WFP and IRC was deployed to the Chadian-Sudanese border on 8 April for an assessment of the humanitarian situation in the area. The Chadian Red Cross and the Territorial Surveillance Brigade (BST) reported that as of 9 April, there were approximately 166 households of 1,860 individuals having crossed the Sudanese border to find refuge in Adré. However, tensions remain high and clashes are still ongoing in El-Geneina, and it is expected that the number of displaced may increase in the coming days, according to local authorities.
