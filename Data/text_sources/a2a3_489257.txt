[18 August 2021, Aleppo, Idleb, NWS] Of the 15 percent of households across NWS who reported living in tents, only 15 percent reported that their tent was less than one year old, leaving a significant 85 percent of households in a vulnerable situation where their tent is more likely to be damaged, less resistant to weather conditions and prone to leakage. Households in Idleb had a higher aggregated rate of living in tents over one year old (87 percent, compared to 79 percent in Aleppo); however, households in Aleppo had higher rates of living in tents that were over two years old (48 percent compared to 30 percent in Idleb).
