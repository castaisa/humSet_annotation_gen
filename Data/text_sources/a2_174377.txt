In NWS (as of 1 October), a total of 1,072 confirmed cases of COVID-19 were reported (401 from Idleb and 671 from Aleppo governorate), including six medically confirmed deaths (Case Fatality Rate: 0.6 per cent). To date, 980 cases developed mild symptoms, 58 developed moderate/severe symptoms, and 34 cases were asymptomatic. A total of 526 (49 per cent) of the cases have already recovered. Among the cases, 629 were males and 443 were females. The mean case age was being 34.7 years, while 10 cases were under five years of age, and 67 cases were over 60 years of age.
