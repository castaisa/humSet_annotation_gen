A total of 86 Community mobilizers and 208 community influencers (religious, youth and community leaders) were oriented on COVID-19 risk communication. Through a partnership programme with 40 radio stations, a total of 6,326 radio jingles on COVID-19 were broadcasted in 10 widely spoken languages in the country and 169 talk shows focusing on COVID-19 and its impact were held. Communication materials focusing on signs and symptoms of COVID-19, prevention and proper use of facemasks were printed, and distribution to partners is ongoing in all the 10 states, with materials printed in 5 languages. During the reporting period, a total of 1,700 posters, 350 banners, 35,000 fliers were distributed
