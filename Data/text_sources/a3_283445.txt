[16 March, 2021, NES] As of 3 March, there have been a total of 67 confirmed COVID-19 cases among IDPs and refugees in camps and sites from a total of 227 samples collected in camps for a positivity rate of 30 per cent (with a further 503 confirmed cases among IDPs and refugees in non-camp settings). The largest number of cases was in Mahmoudli Camp in Ar-Raqqa Governorate, where five individuals tested positive for COVID-19 in the month. As of 16 March, 787 of the total confirmed cases of COVID-19 (9 per cent of all cases) were recorded amongst health workers, with the highest recorded in Al-Hasakeh Governorate.
