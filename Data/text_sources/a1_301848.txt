Rice (High Quality): The average price decreased by 0.1% compared to the second week of November, and remained 8% higher than 14 March. The decrease occurred in: Urozgan(3%), Hilmand and Hirat (1%), due to sustained supply and daily price fluctuations. Meanwhile the price increased in Paktika and Kandahar (1%), due to daily price fluctuations. Prices where highest in Bamyan, Daykundi, Badakhshan and Panjsher (AFN 105, 100, 98 and 98 per kg, respectively), as all of them are most remote areas with high transportation cost. Meanwhile prices were lowest in Urozgan, Kandahar, Ghazni and Hirat (AFN 74, 75, 76 and 78 per kg, respectively).
