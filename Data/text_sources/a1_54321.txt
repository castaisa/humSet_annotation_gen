In this sense, the number of residence permits issued by Chile is noteworthy. It is the group that submitted the largest number of applications for legal residence in the period 2016-2017. The applications were mostly in the Metropolitan Region (75%), followed by Valparaiso Region (7.4%), and Maule Region (3.7%). The profile of the temporary visa applicants is young (almost 80% are between 15-44 years old). In the last two years, more men than women applied for visas. In the last 3 years, about 125,000 residence permits were issued. There was an increase in temporary visas by 273% from 2015 to 2016, and by 316% from 2016 to 2017.
