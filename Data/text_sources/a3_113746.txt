Venezuela: UNHCR continued distributing relief items for improved shelter/water/hygiene for spontaneous returnees in Apure, La Guaira, and Zulia (410 hygiene kits, 500 bars of soap, 100 buckets). In addition, hygiene kits, food and transportation were provided in support of 30 people with special needs and 16 outreach volunteers. As part of the International Day Against Homophobia, Transphobia & Biphobia (IDAHOT), UNHCR and local NGO Orgullo Guayana conducted a virtual training on international protection, protection risks, terminology and safe spaces for regional SSN members in Bolívar. UNHCR also organised two virtual trainings on the identiﬁcation of speciﬁc needs for community leaders from Brisas del Sur and Los Pijiguaos, Bolívar.
