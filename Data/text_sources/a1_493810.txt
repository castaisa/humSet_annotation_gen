[31st Jul 2021, Adamawa State] (Cholera) WASH Water point Chlorination: 64 chlorinators engaged by SI, NRC, IOM and ICRC conducting water point chlorination in 32 water points in Girei and Yola North LGAs. 40 House-to-House Hygiene Promoters engaged by SI, NRC, IOM and ICRC and are conducting hygiene promotions in communities in Girei and Yola North LGAs. Disinfection of Household latrines and public dump sites by IOM and NRC ongoing in communities of Girei and Yola North LGAs. Distribution of IEC materials and Aquatabs at households by ICRC ongoing. Radio jingles on prevention of cholera by IRC and ICRC ongoing.
