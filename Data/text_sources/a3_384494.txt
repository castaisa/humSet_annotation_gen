[15th Jan - 15th Feb 2020, Nigeria] 1E: MOBILITY Most IDPs have been displaced two times and often three times. Fifty-four per cent (down from 57%) of IDPs have been displaced two times in the six most affected north-eastern states. In Borno, 66 per cent (down from 68%) of displaced persons said they have been displaced before. Also, this most-affected state has the lowest percentage (4% - down from 7%) of IDPs who say they have been displaced only once. Adamawa, which has historically been affected by communal clashes, has the highest percentage of people (61%) who say they have been displaced more than three times.
