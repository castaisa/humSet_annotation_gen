UNICEF provided equipment to inc rease the reach of the ’I Learn at Home’ education sessions broadcast in the Masisea district in Ucayali. On 24 June, the Ministry of Education approved the protocol for reopening schools, elaborated with UNICEF support. UNICEF also developed a proposal to inc rease connectivity in 3 regions to reach 74 rural communities and 4 990 students in urban areas, to be shared with potential donors in private sector. Furthermore, UNICEF, UNESCO, WB and IDB finished the strategic plan for reopening schools, which will be presented to the MoE next week and would reach 8.7M students.
