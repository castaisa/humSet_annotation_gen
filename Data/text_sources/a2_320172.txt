[ March 2021, NES, NWS & GoS] Month-on-month (m-o-m), all 14 governorates recorded an increasing average reference food basket price in March 2021. The highest m-o-m increase of 43 percent was reported in Tartous reaching SYP 198,582, while the lowest increase of 24 percent was reported in Ar-Raqqa, reaching SYP 175,503. Idleb continued to report the highest average food basket price at SYP 221,186 (up 26 percent m-o-m). Year-on-year (y-o-y), the price of the reference food basket has significantly increased across all 14 governorates. Aleppo continued to record the highest increase (up 367 percent), while Damascus continued to record the lowest increase (up 242 percent).
