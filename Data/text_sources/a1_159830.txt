In Burkina Faso, the results of the updated Cadre Harmonisé (CH) of July 2020, which focused on updating household food security indicators following the occurrence of COVID- 19, outlined that 3.28 million people are projected to be food security phase 3- 5 across the country. A total of 16 provinces are in phase 3 (crisis) and two provinces in the Sahel region (Soum and Oudalan provinces), are in an emergency situation (phase 4) and need rapid assistance. Pockets of populations in phase 5 (catastrophe) are being reported in both phase 4 provinces (over 5,000 people in Soum and about 6,000 in Oudalan).
