With the 2021 Humanitarian Response Plan, the Shelter/CRI Cluster targeted around 93,000 families out of 203, 198 in need. Despite the lack of funds to respond aqueately to these needs, UNHCR continues to strengthen the shelter response and the distribution of core relief items (CRIs) throughout the country to improve the living conditions, the physical safety and the dignity of displaced populations. So far, only 4% and 2% have been respectively reached by shelter and CRI interventions. Since the start of the year, a total of 3,031 emergency shelters were built in the Center-North, the North and the Sahel regions.
