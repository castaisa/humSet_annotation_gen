Monguno LGA had the next highest rate of households with “high” or “very high” needs in central Borno (34%), with many households exhibiting needs in food security (93%), shelter/NFI (84%), livelihoods (68%), and nutrition (18%). Severity of needs was also high (21.2), with above average severities reported for shelter/NFI (5.7) and food security (5.6). Compared to other LGAs in Central Borno, shelter/NFI needs in Monguno were quite severe (5.7) due to most households having inadequate shelter (79%) and sharing accommodations with multiple families (75%).
