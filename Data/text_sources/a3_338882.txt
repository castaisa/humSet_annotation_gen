[10th - 16th May 2021, Adamawa State] Risk Communication/Social Mobilization: Community Health Champions supported by WHO visited 1,260 households. 7,411 persons were reached. This number comprised of 1,710 men, 2,461 women, 1, 411 boys and 1,829 girls. Broadcast of weekly Community Radio Program by Radio Nigeria Fombina FM and Adamawa State Broadcasting Corporation (ABC) Radio specifically on promoting compliance to safety protocols, voluntary testing, wearing facemask and handwashing is currently ongoing based on the following schedule. Ongoing daily broadcast of 10 jingles per day in Radio Nigeria Fombina FM, 10 jingles per day by Gotel Radio; 10 jingles per day by ABC Radio, 5 jingles per day by Pulaako FM and 5 TV jingles per day in Adamawa State Television.
