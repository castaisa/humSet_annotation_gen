[28 June 2021, Cox's Bazar] Essential Health Service: More than 73 000 ANC-1 services were provided by the public health facilities in Bangladesh in May 2021. When compared to the ANC-1 service utilization in May 2020, utilization in May 2021 is 17% higher. However, the ANC-1 service status in May 2021 has decreased by 6% as compared to that of April 2021. It is important to assess the cause and the alternative strategies adopted to address the decline. About 36 000 PNC-1 services for mothers were provided by public health facilities in May 2021. Compared to April 2021, PNC service utilization has declined by 11% in May 2021. It is important to evaluate the cause and the alternative strategies adopted to address the decline.
