Food Security and Nutrition The main humanitarian needs remain in the sector of Food Security and Nutrition. According to the 2 April GIFMM situation report, there is still a need to guarantee access to three meals per day to all people in the different places of refuge (shelters, streets, and family/friends' homes). A large number of the population under ten years of age represents a nutritional challenge. Although some GIFMM partners are providing two meals in different community kitchens, the food available will not cover the population's needs in the next few days. The Colombian Red Cross Society has also identified the need to provide cash and food vouchers to guarantee access to food and access to prepared and hot foods.
