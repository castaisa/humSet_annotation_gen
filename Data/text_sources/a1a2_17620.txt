Psycho-social support services were provided through the Ministry of Social Affairs and Labour (MOSAL) to 18,815 children and care-givers (1,106 girls, 705 boys, 11,801 women and 5,203 men). • Mine risk awareness sessions run by MOSAL and the country portfolio coordinator to 18,815 people. • Case management services were provided to 146 children (76 girls and 70 boys). • Mine risk education awareness sessions run by MOSAL and the Yemen Executive Mine Action Centre reached 46,778 people (15,336 girls, 14,438 boys, 11,801 women and 5,203 men). • Protection partners continued to provide victim assistance services in Al Hudaydah City and southern districts in the form of medical support to children. •
