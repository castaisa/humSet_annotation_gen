Ibb Hub: During the reporting period humanitarian partners have reported access challenges as they tried to reach conflict-affected districts in Taizz, amid intensified fighting in the western coastal districts particularly Mawzaá and Maqbanah. One humanitarian organisation suspended activities inside Taizz City on 8 May, citing security concerns. Other partners are continuing their operations including WHO which delivered cancer drugs to Taizz and Ibb governorates, as well as cholera supplies to treatment centres and rehydration corners. The agency also distributed diphtheria vaccines and supplies to the two governorates. A WASH assessment conducted by a humanitarian partner in Al Mukha and Dhubab districts in Taizz identifies the need for water trucking and installation of emergency water distribution systems.
