[18 August 2021, NWS] The most frequently reported shelter issue was that of cold and damp conditions, as reported by over half of the households across NWS (54 percent). Lack of heating was the second most frequently reported issue (38 percent), followed by lack of space inside the shelter (33 percent). Cold and damp conditions were found to be especially of concern in finished and unfinished houses and apartments (as reported by 60 and 64 percent of households, respectively), whereas the most frequently reported issue by households living tents is that of a lack of space (61 percent), followed by a lack of privacy (49 percent) and leakages during rain (41 percent).
