Country‐level coordination and response planning: •Health partners continue to support Government-led response to COVID-19. • Humanitarian partners are currently implementing a Humanitarian Response Plan (HRP) which integrates COVID19 needs into the overall response. Of the 14 million people in need of humanitarian assistance, humanitarian partners have prioritised 11.1 million to receive support in 2020, for which US$1.1 billion is required. In the first three quarters of the year, humanitarians were able to reach 7.6 million people with some form of assistance despite a desperate lack of funding. The HRP remains significantly underfunded at just 48.6 per cent of requirements, leaving a gap of $581m.
