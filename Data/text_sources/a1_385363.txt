[5-18 July 2021, Cox's Bazar] Host/Refugee: Since 1 May 2021, there have been 107 cases (88 from Rohingya refugees and 19 from host community) of AWD that have test-ed positive by Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs)/bacteriological culture confirmed cases for cholera (data as of 18 July 2021) bringing the total to 127 (95 from Rohingya refugees and 32 from host community) in 2021. Of the 107 cases, 40 (35 from Rohingya refugees and 5 from host community) have been culture confirmed. In the past three weeks (week 26-28) the total number of culture confirmed cases was 20.
