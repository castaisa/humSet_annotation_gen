[26th January 2021, Bangladesh] Dhaka University’s (DU) Institute of Health Economics (IHE) and Bangladesh Como Modeling Team jointly conducted the study – which had a total of 3,560 participants from 16 upazilas of eight divisions and two Dhaka city corporations – from January 10 to January 25. They also found that around 52% of the respondents did not want to take the vaccine immediately as they were skeptical over the efficacy and safety of the vaccine doses and wanted to wait for a few weeks or months and if possible for a year following the start of the vaccination effort.
