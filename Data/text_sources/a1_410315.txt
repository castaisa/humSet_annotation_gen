[Syria, July] In July 2021, more than one out of five interviewed households (21 percent) reported challenges in accessing medical care services, an increase of six percentage points year-on-year and representing the second highest national average level recorded since April 2020. The lack of access to hospitals and healthcare facilities was more pronounced in Lattakia (28 percent), Al-Hasakeh (28 percent) and Dar'a (25 percent).
