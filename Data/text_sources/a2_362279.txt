[10th July 2021, Nigeria]There are already fears that the sudden outbreak of cholera may complicate Nigeria’s fight against the COVID-19 pandemic as cases which had slowed since December 2020 are now rising steeply. The number of confirmed COVID-19 cases rose from 40 on July 2 to 186 on July 9, 2021, raising the total number of cases in the country to 168,442 with 2,122 deaths and 164,423 recoveries. With the Delta variant recorded in Nigeria which the NCDC described as worrisome, there are fears that it could destabilise the overstretched health professionals and derail Nigeria’s earlier success in tackling the virus.
