[January 07 ,Lattakia]The Director of Education, Imran Abu Khalil, confirmed that the first day of the exam took place in accordance with the ministerial health protocol to confront the Corona virus, from temperature measurement, physical distancing, cleaning and sterilization before the exam, which will take place after each subject, noting that the student turnout was good and distinguished, and no case with symptoms of Corona was received, pointing The governorate schools are fully prepared for the first semester exams, indicating that 268,000 male and female students have taken the exam from the first grade of primary to the third secondary school.
