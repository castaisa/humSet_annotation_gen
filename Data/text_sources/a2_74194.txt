Response strategy The response approach aims at guaranteeing food and nutrition in crisis situations, including different strategies to reduce/eliminate food insecurity, provide nutrition and save lives. The geographical areas of first priority will be the border departments of La Guajira, Norte de Santander, Arauca and Nariño, followed by Atlántico, Magdalena, Bolívar, Cesar, Antioquia and Valle del Cauca. The estimation of people in need and targets for 2020 were based on most recent indicators and evaluations, which identified the population groups in the most vulnerable situation and areas with the greatest needs in terms of food security. Additionally, geographic areas and objectives reached in terms of coverage and impact of the interventions during 2018 and 2019 were analysed in order to replicate and scale up the response in 2020
