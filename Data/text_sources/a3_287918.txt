[26 Feb, 2021, NWS] As of 26 February, the total number of COVID-19 associated deaths were 408, with a case fatality rate of 1.93 %. The mean age of COVID-19 cases was 37.4 years, with 53 cases reported to be under the age of five and 2,172 (10.3%) cases reported to be over the age of 60. Since the first case of COVID-19 was confirmed on 9 July, by 25 February some 94,000 samples had been tested via RT-PCR, with a total test positivity rate of 24.0%. As of 25 January, the number of health care workers (HCWs) and health facilities staff (physicians, dentists, nurses, midwives and various medical technicians) who contracted COVID-19 disease reached 1,554 (7.4 percent) and another 1,136 (5.4 percent) were other staff working in healthcare facilities or community health workers.
