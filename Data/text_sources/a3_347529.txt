[10-23 may, 2021, Cox's Bazar]Refugee/Host: Since the onset of the outbreak in 2017, Diphtheria surveillance is ongoing in the camps. The total number of diphtheria cases reported is 9274 to date (3016 in 2017; 5330 in 2018; 614 in 2019; 226 in 2020 and 88 as of week 20, 2021). In total, 9029 cases were reported in the camps and 245 from the host community, with 47 deaths registered in the refugee camps and none in the host community. While the first Diphtheria case was detected on 10 November 2017, the first death occurred on 29 December 2017 and the last death on 25 October 2019.
