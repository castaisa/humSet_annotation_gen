Regardless of displacement status or location, the average severity of WASH needs across South Sudan remained, as in Round 23 and Round 24, at Level 4 (Alert). Throughout the country, 50 counties were classified as Level 4, 27 as Level 3 and only one, Magwi, in Level 2. Nevertheless, important differences were observed between population groups, settings, and regions and the different composite indicators. Great Bahr el Ghazal (GBeG) 11 and Greater Upper Nile (GUN) 12 also had severity rankings of Level 4 (averaging 3.8 and 3.7 respectively), though the severity per indicator varied by states. Greater Equatoria (GE) had the lowest overall severity ranking at 3.4.(December 2019)
