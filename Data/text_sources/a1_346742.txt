(Whole of Syria, April 2021) The national average price of WFP’s standard reference food basket decreased between March and April 2021 by two percent, reaching SYP 176,471 (USD 71 at the official exchange rate of SYP 2,500/USD). This is the first month-on-month (m-o-m) decrease in the food basket price since August 2020, and it is linked to the improvement in the informal exchange rate by 26 percent between March and April 2021. Eight governorates recorded a decreasing average reference food basket price in April 2021. The highest m-o-m decrease of 17 percent was re-ported in Idleb reaching SYP 183,563, followed by Al-Hasakeh (down 16 percent), reaching SYP 127,870. The remaining six governorates recorded an increasing reference food basket price.
