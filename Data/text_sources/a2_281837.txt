[Survey in NW, SW, Littoral and West region, conducted on 4,638 people through one to one interview using a survey tool] Overall, 94% of respondents report that they have tried to prevent or prepare for COVID19. This is equal for both men and women surveyed. The percentage of respondents reporting having tried to prevent or prepare is similar in the NW (94%) and SW (93%). However, respondents in the Littoral were slightly lesslikely to have tried to prevent or prepare (only 91% reported doing so) and those in the West slightly more likely to have done so (98% report doing so).
