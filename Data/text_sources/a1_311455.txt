(Sahel) UNHCR and CONAREF facilitated the relocation of 1,211 refugees and asylum-seekers from the town of Djibo to Goudoubo refugee camp, while respecting all COVID-19 preventative measures. These returns come in addition to the 5,250 refugees relocated to the site from the city of Dori since last December. UNHCR and its partners supported them with 490 emergency shelters. As part of efforts to mitigate the spread of COVID-19, UNHCR’s health partners distributed 115 hand-washing devices and 100 cartons of soap. In addition, 500 families, including 440 from the refugee and 60 from the host communities, were supported each with 10 goats to rebuild their livestock and foster livelihood opportunities.
