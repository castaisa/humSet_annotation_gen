Another important factor affecting the health sector is the lack of specialists who can provide the services and care required by the population. For instance, in the most affected areas, a 500-bed hospital that previously offered up to twenty medical specialties is now only able to offer five due to the lack of medical specialists. The shortage of necessary resources, supplies and equipment aggravates this situation. At the same time, the population’s needs are on the rise, as are the number of patients who check in to the ER a consequence of violence. For all these reasons, the ICRC is focused on working closely with health centres, in order to save more lives.
