[20th February 2021, Bangladesh] The successful completion of the first two weeks of the nationwide Covid-19 program demonstrates that the campaign has gained the trust of the general people, who have overcome their initial fears surrounding the vaccine. Health experts, virologists and physicians are of the opinion that despite this being the first mass level adult vaccination in the country, the process is going on without a hitch as Bangladesh already had the Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI) in place. Bangladesh initiated the EPI with six conventional vaccines — BCG, DPT, OPV, TT and Measles — at the outset in 1979.
