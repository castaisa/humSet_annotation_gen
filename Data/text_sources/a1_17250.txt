FOOD DISTRIBUTION ◼ During the reporting period, a total of 28,135 conflict affected households (196,917 persons) in Shabwa, Sada’a, Al-Mahweet, and Sana’a City received food assistance consisting of 60kg of rice, 25kg of beans, 25kg of lentils, 5kg sugar, 3kg tomato paste, 10L sunflower oil, 1kg salt, and 30 sachets of the nutritional supplement “plumpy sup”. In addition, and as a measure of promoting good hygiene promotion, each household received 10 soaps; ◼ In addition, 500 food parcels were donated to the Al-Mahra branch of the Yemen Red Crescent Society (YRCS) to support those affected by cyclone Luban.
