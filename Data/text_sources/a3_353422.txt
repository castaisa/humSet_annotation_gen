The health promotion messages were centered on COVID-19 second (2nd) dose vaccination awareness, personal and environmental hygiene. To further support the Borno state government to successfully roll out the 2nd dose of the COVID-19 vaccine, the IRC provided support through training of 64 (49 M, 15F) MOH staff on COVID-19 vaccination and IPC measures in Monguno and Gwoza LGAs, supply of IPC materials, provision of Incentive for state supervision team, and widespread health education and promotion activities through paid radio jingles, banners and fliers, and community message dissemination by town criers.
