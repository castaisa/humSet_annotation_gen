[May 2021, Cox's Bazar] Refugees: 15 more Rohingya were detained in the Cox’s Bazar camps after they had returned from Bhashan Char without permission. On 24 May, three Rohingya (including two women) were reported to have returned to Camp 22 after fleeing from Bhashan Chair by paying brokers.18 Again, on 27 May, a 40- year-old man was detected in Camp 26 who had also fled Bhashan Char.19 On 29 May, ten Rohingya were detained by the Armed Police Battalion (APBn) in Camp 3 for moving from Bhashan Char without permission.20 Finally, on 30 May, a 15-year-old Rohingya boy was detained in the Kutupalong area for fleeing from the island.
