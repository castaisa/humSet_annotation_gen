More than a third of the population lives in a single room (36%) and half of the popu- lation shares a room with one or two other people (51%); to a lesser extent, they were in overcrowded conditions, sharing a room with three or more people (13%). Overcrowding among the displaced population in Honduras is worrying, reaching a third of the population (34%), and among refugees or asylum-seekers in Colombia, which exceeds half of the popu- lation (57%), as well as among migrants, who in this country exceed a ﬁfth of the population (22%), similar to the percentage of refugees or asylum-seekers in Peru (20%).
