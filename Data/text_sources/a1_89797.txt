Acute Respiratory Infection: In Epi week 4, 6,872 cases of acute respiratory infection were reported through EWARS. Of the reported cases, 263 were from ICRC FSP Clinic in Monguno, 254 were from General Hospital Ngala (FHI360), 231 were from FHI360 clinic Banki in Bama, 225 were from Damboa MCH in Damboa, 206 were from Shuwari Host Community Clinic in Damboa, 191 were from ICRC GGSS IDP Camp Clinic in Monguno, 185 cases each from 1000 Housing Estate clinic Dikwa and INTERSOS Health Facility Gamboru in Ngala and 184 were from Sabon Gari Lowcost IDP camp clinic (MDM) in Damboa. No associated death was reported.
