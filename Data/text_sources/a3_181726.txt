[19-25th Oct 2020, Cox's Bazar]  A total of 1416 community health workers (CHWs) were trained to provide enhanced Community Based Surveillance and Home-Based Care which includes counselling on testing, quarantine and patients' referral to isolation facilities. During the reporting period CHWs conducted 142 678 household visits in which 3095 patients were identified with mild respiratory symptoms (fever, sore throat, cough) and 22 patients with moderate/severe symptoms. The cumulative number of mild patients is 45 838, and 199 moderate/ severe patients. During the reporting period, 1376 persons with COVID symptoms were referred to health facilities from a total of 21 045 to date
