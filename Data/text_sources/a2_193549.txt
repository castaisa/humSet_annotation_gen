[1st Sep 2020, NorthEast Nigeria]Among IDPs living in camps and camp-like settings, most sites reportedly had access to soap and water at 39 per cent (up from 34%). In a sign of improvement, about half the people had access to soap and water in 18 per cent of sites (up from 16%), a few respondents had access to soap and water in 17 per cent (down from 22%) of sites. Everyone in 14 per cent (down from 15%) of sites reportedly had access to soap and water, and nobody in 12 per cent (down from 13%) of sites had access.
