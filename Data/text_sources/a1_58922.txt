One of the few to help on the road TDH was one of the first and still is one of the few organisations along the road that connects Cucuta with Bucaramanga. Our social workers identify the most vulnerable cases, so that they can get support. To help them survive, we provide them with warm clothes, food vouchers, baby nappies, hygiene kits including toilet paper and toothbrushes, or sometimes cash so they can buy any urgent supplies. We also coordinate with our partner NGO SOS Children’s Villages, which covers the second part of the road, so they can bring the most vulnerable to their centre in Bucaramanga where families can spend the night and get access to transport.
