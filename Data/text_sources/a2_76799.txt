A total of 81 suspected cases have been reported so far in 2019 of which 39 confirmed cases were recorded in nine states (Bayelsa, Lagos, Delta, Rivers,Akwa ibom, Enugu, Anambra, Cross River, and Oyo) and one death • Of the confirmed cases, 59% are from two states, Delta (28%) and Lagos (26%) • The most affected age-group is 21-40 years (Range: 15-51years, median age: 32 years) • 84.6% of confirmed cases are male (male to female ratio = 2.6:1) • Since the beginning of the outbreak in September 2017, 176 confirmed cases and nine deaths have been recorded in 18 states (Rivers, Bayelsa, Cross River, Imo, Akwa Ibom, Lagos, Delta, Bauchi, FCT, Abia, Oyo, Enugu, Ekiti, Nasarawa, Benue, Plateau, Edo,Anambra)
