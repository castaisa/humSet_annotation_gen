Inside Venezuela, the Pan American Health Organization is cooperating with the Venezuelan Ministry of Health to strengthen the health system and provide medicines, vaccines, and other medical supplies (PAHO 09/2018). In late 2018, UNICEF also has an agreement with the government for supplying medicines, health, and nutrition supplies for women and children inside Venezuela (UNICEF 26/11/2018). Between 2018 and 2019, the International Committee of the Red Cross has more than doubled their budget for responding to the crisis inside the country, from 9 million CHF to 19 million CHF. Their focus is for the most part on the areas of health and sanitation (ICRC 01/03/2019).
