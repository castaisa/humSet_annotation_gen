[5-13th April 2021, NWS] In April, the value of the regional Survival Minimum Expenditure Basket (SMEB) dropped slightly to 411,125 SYP, a 3% decrease compared to March, although a 74% long-term increase since October 2020, hovering just below the highest ever recorded price in the Market Monitoring Exercise in NWS, set last month. The SMEB in United States Dollar (USD) increased slightly from 113 USD in March to 116 USD in March alongside a slight depreciation of the SYP against the USD. The average SMEB value in USD has remained - between 113 and 116 USD - since the beginning of 2021, however this is the highest cost in USD since May 2019, two years ago.
