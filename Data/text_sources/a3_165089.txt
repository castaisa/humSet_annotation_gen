The COVID-19 pandemic affected uptake of essential health services and coverage rates across the states due to lockdown measures and reluctance of health care workers in providing services stemming from concern of infection. At the state level, UNICEF is supporting integrated routine immunization outreaches in poor performing LGAs, to improve coverage and actively participating in and supporting online coordination platforms to strengthen service delivery, while following COVID-19 protocols. Logistics support is also being provided for routine immunization supportive supervision. In Kano, State Level Vaccine Management Training concluded, with 250 participants trained and the training of 2360 frontline health workers across the 44 LGAs is currently ongoing. In Katsina state, 42,391 children were administered measles reactive vaccination.
