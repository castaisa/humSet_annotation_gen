[23rd June 2021, Cox's Bazar] The overall weighted prevalence of global chronic malnutrition or Stunting per HAZ among children 6-59 months was 23.7% [20.4 - 27.3 95% C.I.] of which 20.7% [16.6 - 25.6 95% C.I.] in Ukhiya and 25.8% [20.8 - 31.6 95% C.I.] in Teknaf Upazila that are considered ‘High” as per WHO/UNICEF latest classification. The overall weighted severe stunting prevalence was found to be 3.0% [1.9 - 4.8 95% C.I.] with an estimate of 3.2% [1.7 - 5.9 95% C.I.] in Ukhiya and 3.1% [1.7 - 5.4 95% C.I.] in Teknaf Upazila. There is no significant different observed in global, moderate and severe stunting prevalence between the two upazilas [P>0.05].
