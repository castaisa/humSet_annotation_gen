Based on our regular monitoring of the 31 informal sites mapped as hotspots for evictions since January 2020, the sub-sector has recorded an increase in evictions from 30 per cent to 50 per cent, as exemplified by these six cases registered in MMC and Jere LGAs within a span of one month as opposed to the regular one or two cases received per month in the previous months. This is attributable to inability of the tenants (IDPs) to pay rent, further aggravated by COVID-19 related economic hardships; the lack of incentives for landowners to use of their land; and the need for landowners to use their land for other profitable purposes.
