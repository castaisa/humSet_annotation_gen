In 67% of baladiyas IDPs purchased food from the market as their main source of food, representing a two percent decrease from the previous round. The proportion of IDPs obtaining food on credit was reported to be 16% representing a one percent decrease from the previous round. In 16% of baladiyas the main source of food was reported to be from charity or donations with 1 baladiya reporting family or friends as the main source of food (Rigaldeen). Food was reported as too expensive in 94 assessed baladiyas. Cases of malnutrition were reported in 13% of baladiyas and were reported to be present in the south, east and west of the country
