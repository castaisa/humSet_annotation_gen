Health authorities in the departments of La Guajira, Norte de Santander and Antioquia strengthened through 9 additional hospital staff and medical professionals, and 22 pieces of communications equipment and computers. Infrastructure improvements and over 11,600 distinct items provided to 10 public hospitals and health institutions, including ventilators, biomedical supplies for Intensive Care Units (ICU), triage, emergency and hospitalization services. 15,669 Personal Protective Items (PPE), including surgical N95 and face masks, disposable gowns, face shields, eye protection and goggles, sterile and non-sterile gloves, leggings, infrared digital thermometers, hand soaps, surface soaps, cleaning and disinfecting materials, donated to public hospitals, health institutions and state institutions.
