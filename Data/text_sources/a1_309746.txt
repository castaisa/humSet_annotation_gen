About 80% of the counties in IPC AMN Phase 4 (Critical) are in the Greater Upper Nile State followed by 17% in Greater Bahr el Ghazal State. 25 counties mainly in Greater Equatoria are in IPC AMN Phase 2 (Alert) and below. Of the counties in IPC AMN Phase 4 (Critical), nearly 80% are in Greater Upper Nile State followed by Greater Bahr el Ghazal State. Counties in Jonglei State (31%), Upper Nile State (31%), Unity State (17%), and Warrap State (10%) and parts of Eastern Equatoria State (3%), Northern Bahr el Ghazal State (3%) and Lakes State (3%) are in IPC AMN Phase 4 (Critical).
