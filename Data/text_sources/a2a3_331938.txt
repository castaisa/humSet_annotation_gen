[March 2021, NES & GoS] The availability of cattle feed for milk production was generally acceptable in the monitored sites, with an average price of 1 220 SYP per kg recorded in March 2021 (23 percent increase m-o-m). The highest selling price was observed in both Hasakeh and Deir-ez-Zor at 1 800 SYP per kg, while the lowest price was recorded in Rural Damascus governorate at 1 100 SYP per kg, Aleppo governorate witnessed a significant increase in the average price of cattle feed for milk production with a 46 percent m-o-m increase, with the price reaching 1 400 SYP per Kg. Cattle feed for meat production (i.e. compound fodder pellets) was available across all the monitored governorates at an estimated price of 1 161 SYP per kg. Deir-ez-Zor recorded the highest price of cattle feed, which was sold for at least 1 800 SYP per kg.
